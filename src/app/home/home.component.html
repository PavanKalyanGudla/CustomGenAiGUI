User Experience Services

Gen AI Services For You

Chat GPT Bot

To use the Generative AI ChatGPT service, simply open the chat application, type your question or message into the chat box, and press "Send." The AI will respond with an answer, which you can read and continue the conversation as needed. It's like chatting with a virtual assistant that can help with a variety of tasks.

Image Generation

To use the image generation service, open the application, input a description of the image you want, and press "Generate." The AI will create an image based on your description, which you can view and download. It's like describing a scene to an artist who quickly brings it to life.

Image Analysis

To use the image analysis service, upload an image to the application and press "Analyze." The AI will examine the image and provide insights, such as identifying objects, text, or patterns within it. It's like having a smart assistant that can "see" and interpret images for you.

Translator

To use the translator service, open the application, enter the text you want to translate, select the target language, and press "Translate." The AI will provide the translated text instantly. It's like having a personal language expert at your fingertips.

Resumae Analysis

To use the resume analysis service, upload your resume to the application and press "Analyze." The AI will review your resume and provide feedback on areas like skills, experience, and formatting. It's like having a professional resume reviewer give you instant, tailored advice.
